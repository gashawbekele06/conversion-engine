## CS-01 — Fintech Series B, ~60 employees

**Engagement.** Dedicated 5-engineer Python/data squad, 12 months.

**Starting state.** In-house recruiting backlogged 4+ months on senior
Python; fresh Series B capital with urgency to ship a new lending product.

**Outcome.** Squad onboarded in 11 business days. Shipped MVP of new
product in 5 months vs. internal 9-month target. Retained squad through
a 6-month extension.

**Quotable metric.** "Time-to-first-shipped-PR was 9 days."

---

## CS-02 — Mid-market e-commerce platform, ~950 employees, post-layoff

**Engagement.** 8-engineer hybrid (Python backend + Go infra) replacing
a 14-person in-house team reduction, 18 months.

**Starting state.** 20% headcount reduction; need to preserve delivery
capacity on checkout and fulfilment services without a visible regression.

**Outcome.** Net delivery throughput +6% in first quarter post-transition
(measured via deployment-frequency and change-failure-rate). Cost per
engineer 48% lower than in-house loaded cost.

**Quotable metric.** "Delivery throughput held within 95% CI of pre-RIF
baseline by week 9."

---

## CS-03 — Seed-to-A ML tooling company, ~30 employees

**Engagement.** 3-engineer ML platform project, 4 months.

**Starting state.** Mono-model inference stack about to buckle under
multi-model product launch; in-house team had no MLOps experience.

**Outcome.** Delivered model-serving + evaluation platform (Ray + MLflow
+ custom eval harness). Launch proceeded on schedule. Tenacious team
transitioned to a retained 1-engineer support contract.

**Quotable metric.** "Model-serving p95 latency reduced from 480ms to
140ms on identical hardware."
